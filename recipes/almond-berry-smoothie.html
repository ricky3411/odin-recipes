Almond Berry Smoothie

Description

Want something to drink that is tasty but also healthy? Well the almond berry smoothie is the perfect choice!
A creamy beverage that is made of blueberry and almond ingredients to give you the taste of both worlds!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter

Steps

  1. Combine blueberries, banana, almond milk, and almond butter in a blender; blend until smooth. (Add water for a thinner smoothie.)
