Garlic Bread

Description

Liked the taste of garlic and bread? Well why not put them together and have a flavorful delicious tasting meal!
Garlic bread is a bread that contains garlic or butter and is served either baked or toasted.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • ground black pepper
  • 1/4 large baguette, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill to heat.
  2. Mix together butter, garlic, Parmesan cheese, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Spread the butter mixture generously on the baguette slices.
  4. Place slices on the preheated grill and cook until butter is melted and bread is lightly brown, about 30 seconds
  5. Sprinkle with parsley and server immediately
